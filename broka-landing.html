Simple Process

How BROKA works

Four steps from listing to paid — with an AI mediator protecting you at every turn.

STEP 01
📝

List or Discover

Post your item in 60 seconds with photos, video proof, and a price. Or browse verified listings filtered by location, category, and price.

STEP 02
🤖

AI Broker Steps In

Zeno — BROKA's AI powered by Llama 3.3 70B — checks seller credibility, compares market prices, detects fraud risk, and coaches both sides toward a fair deal.

STEP 03
🤝

Negotiate in Your Language

Chat directly or via AI-assisted mode in English, Swahili, Sheng, Luo, Kikuyu, or Luganda. Voice notes powered by natural East African TTS voices.

STEP 04
✅

Pay Securely via M-Pesa

Funds are held in escrow with biometric confirmation before release. If anything goes wrong, BROKA's dispute system resolves it fairly and fast.

What makes BROKA different

Built for East Africa.
Not adapted for it.

Every feature was designed from the ground up for Kenyan and East African commerce — not retrofitted from a Western template.

🛡️

Escrow Protection

Funds are held until the buyer confirms delivery. Neither party can run with the money. Full audit trail on every deal.

🎯

Zeno AI Price Analysis

Zeno compares your listing against live market data, scores the seller's credibility, and gives both parties an unbiased fair-price verdict.

🔐

Biometric Payment Auth

Every M-Pesa payment requires a fresh fingerprint or face scan — captured live, not stored device data — so no one approves a payment but you.

Verified Photo & Video Proof

Sellers upload verified media — photos and video walkthroughs — so you know exactly what you're buying before you meet.

⚡

STK Push Payments

M-Pesa payment requests land directly on your phone. No USSD menus, no copy-pasting. One tap to pay, one tap to confirm.

Live Auctions

Run timed bidding events on your listings. Buyers compete in real time, you get the best price, and BROKA manages the whole process.

Dispute Resolution
